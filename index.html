Level Up

Developed by Jordan Stone, Rajiv Tiwari, Odis Clemons, and Donaldson Roessling

Elevator Pitch

Looking for a distraction? Want to waste some time? Look no further. Go to Level Up and play a wide variety of mini-games, challenge others to beat your highscore, and chat with other gamers.

Concept

Level Up is a mini-game platform with numerous popular games to choose from.

Save favorite games after sigining up with an account.

Chat with other users through a chat box.

Challenges

Heroku pushing and proper routing.

Learning how to use socket.io and configuring it to make a chatroom.

Adding the games because they all have different methods of displaying game elements on the screen. For example, some games use html canvas, some games use absolute positioned elements for different things, etc.
